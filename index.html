Who We Are

About KARAS Foundation

KARAS Foundation is established for the holistic development of students in Vizianagaram District. We provide scholarships and comprehensive educational support to underprivileged and meritorious learners, with a special focus on rural areas, girl child education, and differently-abled students.

Our approach has always been a holistic development of communities deprived of their rights and basic needs — empowering through compassion, educating through innovation, and transforming through collective action.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, knowledge-driven Vizianagaram where no student's potential is lost to poverty, geography, gender, or disability — where meritorious and underprivileged learners alike receive scholarships, recognition, and the tools to advance through quality education, digital access, and skill-based opportunity.

Our Mission

To ensure equitable access to quality education through financial aid, learning infrastructure, digital tools, career guidance, and skill development, enabling every student to achieve academic recognition and advancement.

Education

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We provide scholarships, free textbooks, uniforms, and essential learning materials to economically underprivileged students to enable continuity of education. We also extend financial aid, coaching, and mentorship to meritorious students for higher studies, competitive examinations, research, and innovation.

Technology

Digital Education & E-Learning

We facilitate access to digital education, e-learning resources, and technology-enabled teaching to bridge the urban-rural divide. Our digital classrooms, libraries, and reading centers bring modern education to rural areas where access was previously limited.

Empowerment

Girl Child Education

We promote girl child education and empowerment through specialized support, aids, and inclusive learning opportunities. Our programs are designed to ensure gender equity in education and empower young girls to become self-reliant leaders of tomorrow.

View Programs
Livelihoods

Skill Development & Career Guidance

We organize career guidance and counseling sessions, conduct free coaching for competitive exams, and offer vocational and skill-development training for livelihood creation. Our goal is to help every student achieve economic stability and self-reliance.

View Programs
Community

Community Development

Our holistic approach serves tribal, backward classes, and downtrodden communities in rural and urban areas. We collaborate with government schools, private institutions, and local communities for educational reforms, teacher support, and awareness campaigns on the importance of education.
