Who We Are

Empowering Through Education

KARAS Foundation is established for the holistic development of students in Vizianagaram District. We provide scholarships and comprehensive educational support to underprivileged and meritorious learners, with a special focus on rural areas, girl child education, and differently-abled students.

Our approach has always been a holistic development of communities deprived of their rights and basic needs — empowering through compassion, educating through innovation, and transforming through collective action.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, knowledge-driven Vizianagaram where no student's potential is lost to poverty, geography, gender, or disability — where meritorious and underprivileged learners alike receive scholarships, recognition, and the tools to advance through quality education, digital access, and skill-based opportunity.

Our Mission

To ensure equitable access to quality education through financial aid, learning infrastructure, digital tools, career guidance, and skill development, enabling every student to achieve academic recognition and advancement.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Comprehensive programs designed to uplift communities and transform lives through education and empowerment.

Education & Scholarships

Providing scholarships, free textbooks, uniforms, and essential learning materials to underprivileged students for continuity of education.

Career Guidance

Organizing career guidance and counseling sessions, conducting free coaching classes for competitive exams and higher studies.

Digital Learning

Facilitating access to digital education, e-learning resources, and technology-enabled teaching to bridge the urban-rural divide.

Girl Child Education

Promoting girl child education and empowerment through specialized support and inclusive learning opportunities.

Skill Development

Offering vocational training and skill-development programs for livelihood creation and economic stability.

Community Development

Holistic development of tribal, backward classes, and downtrodden communities in rural and urban areas across Andhra Pradesh.
